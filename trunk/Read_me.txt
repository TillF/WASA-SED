WASA-SED Model
--------------

WASA-SED-model with documentation, sample data set and source code.
(all rights under the stated license (see license.txt) of the WASA-SED code is with the SESAM-Project c/o Andreas Guentner, Deutsches Geoforschungszentrum Potsdam, Telegrafenberg, 14473 Potsdam, Germany, please contact: guentner@gfz-potsdam.de)

******************************
WASA-SED is a large and complex hydrological and sediment transport model. It is not a polished point-and-click GUI tool. Extensive knowledge of its design, purpose, and limitations is required in order to apply it properly. See license.txt for terms of use.
******************************
The source-code (rev. 134) has successfully been compiled with Compaq Visual Fortran 6.6, Intel Fortran XE 14.0, gfortran 4.4 and pgfortran 10.9-0.
Please see Makefile* for necessary flags.
